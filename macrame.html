What Is Macrame?

Macrame is a bit fun and unique in the way that it isn't about the product used to create, but rather the technique and
texture that is created after using said technique. Macrame is achieved using two primary types of knots:

  • Square(Reef Knot)
  • Hitching
Macrame has been around since as early as the 16th century but really gained its popularity
in Genoa during the 19th century. Macrame can be created using a wide variety of materials. Some of the most popular being:
  • Cotton Twine
  • Linen
  • Hemp
  • Jute
  • Leather
  • Yarn
