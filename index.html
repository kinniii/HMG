About My Shop

Hello and welcome to my little shop, Handmade Guided! For as long as I can remember
I have always had such a love for DIY and crafting. One funny issue I found myself running into is...
"Where do I put all of this stuff when I'm done making it??" So I decided to open a small shop just to share
and invite anyone to enjoy the crafts that I love to make so much! I specifically enjoy making macrame style crafts!
Thanks so much for stopping by my website and feel free to contact me for any custom orders or questions I can help with!
